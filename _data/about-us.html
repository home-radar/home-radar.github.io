About our ratings

Knowledge from us to you

Our rating system is based on reports and reviews of maintenance, management, pest control, and general happiness of tenants. Three stars ★★★ means there are none (or very, very few) negative reports. The quality of the apartment is very high for most tenants. Two stars ★★☆ means negative reports exist, but there are also some positives. These apartments often have a mixed bag of reports. One star ★☆☆ means there are serious health, safety, cleanliness, or security problems with the apartment that impede many tenants.
